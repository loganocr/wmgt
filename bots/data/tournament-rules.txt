# Rules of The Weekly

* **Username Consistency**: Scorecard and Discord name must match
* **Start on Time**: strive for :00 minutes on the scorecard ! (show up 3 minutes early). 
* **No Solo or Proxy Play Allowed**
* **No Mulligans**: Play the ball as it lies
* **Complete All 18 Holes**: Incomplete scorecards will not be accepted
* **Submit Scorecard Promptly**: Upload your scorecard within 15 minutes of finishing
* **Respect Other Players**: Keep chat friendly and sportsmanlike
* **Report Issues Immediately**: If you encounter a bug or problem, notify a tournament admin

By registering, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in score disqualification or removal from the tournament.

For full details, visit the tournament website.
