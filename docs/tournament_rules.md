# Rules of The Weekly (WMGT)

1. Username Consistency: Scorecard and Discord name must match
1. Start on Time: strive for :00 minutes on the scorecard ! (show up 3 minutes early). 
1. No Solo or Proxy Play Allowed
1. Stroke Limit must be ON
1. Respect Other Players (read our Code of Conduct Policy)
1. Always play all 36 holes
1. Take a picture, of both scorecards, with the time stamp showing
1. Post the pictures of your scorecard to the #wmgt-scorecards channel
1. Always Enter Your Scores right away: Scoring closes automatically
1. Once signups close, absolutely no changes
1. Have Fun & be polite, and adhere to our Code of Conduct 



Read below for clarification of these rules.

1. Username Consistency

Usernames need to be consistent everywhere in order to communicate with you and keep everything organized.

Discord Display Name must be used:

- On this website
- On Discord
- In the game on your WMG player profile (so it matches on the scorecard and everywhere else)

If a signup name cannot be found on the The Classic Golf League discord server, it will be removed. Join the server at https://discord.gg/theclassicgolfleague



2. Start on Time

The goal time on the Easy scorecard is :00 mins past the hour. The second course must follow no more than 2 min after the first course.

This also ensures that there are absolutely no round re-starts.

Rounds with a late start time may be disqualified.

- Players can't be added to a room if they didn't signup
- You may not change times
- You may not change rooms
- Repeat NO SHOWS may be handled the same as when players fail to enter their scores

Joining late is not allowed.  (It's not only disruptive, but can cause other issues with certain courses)
If a player isn't there, start without them



3. No Solo or Proxy Play Allowed

All tournament rounds must be played with at least one other player present - playing alone is never permitted.

**Single Player Time Slots:** If, at the time registration closes, a time slot has only a single player, that player will need to find an admin or an admin-designated player to join them to play their round. Otherwise, they will be assigned, by and admin, to a different time slot.

**During Tournament Play:** If it's :00 and you're alone, join the last room listed in your time slot

Don't wait or tag players asking if they're coming
The longer you wait, the more likely to disrupt the room you join



4. Stroke Limit must be ON

If you're unable to finish a hole in the alloted stroke limit for the hole, there's no sense in continuing hitting the ball around.  The Stroke Limit feature must be turned on for tournament play.



5. Respect Other Players

- Familiarize yourself with the Code of Conduct
- Don't waste people's time (this includes looking for balls, fox-hunt clues, or recording your shots)
- Try your best
- Be ready when it's your turn
- Do NOT take excessive practice swings that seem to take 20 to 30 seconds
- Minimize talk during player's shots
- Stay out of sight/up in the air
- Watch your language
- Be encouraging
- Poor gamesmanship and hoping for others to fail is not welcome



6. Always play all 36 holes
 

Any hole that is not finished will receive the par of the hole plus 4 strokes.  No exceptions.

If you run out of battery before finishing, anything incomplete will be par plus 4 strokes.

7. Take a picture, of both scorecards, with the time stamp showing

Only one set of pictures from your room need to be posted in the #scorecard-submission channel. However, it is strongly recommended that everyone take their own picture to avoid problems.

Remember, **no picture** __with timestamp__, **no score.**

Cards must have the time stamp visible.  This is exposed by clicking the 3 dots in the bottom right corner of the card
All cards should be clear and cropped.

Although only one player from each room needs to post the scorecard screenshots, ALL players are responsible for entering their OWN scores.
 



8. Post the pictures of your scorecard

Scorecard pictures with a time stamp showing must be posted per room to the #wmgt-scorecards channel.



9. Always Enter Your Scores right away
 

Enter scores immediately after your rounds are over.  (Posting a picture is not the same as entering scores!)

Thr ability to **enter scores** for each group **closes automatically** __4 hrs__ after their start time. ⚠️

The scores will be validated via pictures posted to #wmgt-⁠scorecards



We take score submission very seriously because points are calculated based on everyone's participation. Not entering your scores may result in the following:

- Penalty of -1 Season Point for not entering scores the first time
- 2nd Offense is a 4 Week Mandatory Break from the event
- 3rd Offense is Disqualification from future participation

The repeat offenses may not be confined to the current season; it takes a full season w/o any issues to clear incidents
 

A par +4 is given for any hole that the scorecards cannot confirm.



10. Once signups close, absolutely no changes

The start of the tournament is signaled by the closing of signups.  Signups close 1 hour before the tournament starts (4pm Central | 5pm Eastern | 21:00 UTC)

Once signups close:

Players **can't** be added to a room if they didn't signup. Just don't ask.
You may **not** change times.
You may **not** change rooms (except as listed on 3.)
If you can't make your time, tag a Tournament Admin in #wmgt-chat and let them know


11. Have Fun & Be Polite

Some Examples of unacceptable behavior  (and breaking the rules):

- Using aim-bots, pool shots, etc.
- Restarting a course for any reason
- Quitting rounds
- Using offensive language. If you're not sure, don't say it
- Being a visual or auditory distraction
- Smacking the ball around and trying nonsensical shots
- Not showing when signing up
- Showing up late
- Not entering your scores
- Leaving a course before players can get a pic of the cards
